A day in the life

What an overnight
looks like.

Every overnight follows your dog's usual routine — just with a familiar, trusted face keeping things going.

01
7:00 AM
Morning Walk & Breakfast
We start the day with a proper walk at your dog's pace, on your dog's route. Breakfast served exactly as you've specified.
02
9:00 AM
Morning Photo Update
A quick text with a photo so you know how the morning is going. You'll always hear from me — no news isn't good news here.
03
Midday
Playtime & Rest
Depending on your dog's energy: yard time, indoor play, or a peaceful afternoon nap. Their call, always.
04
5:30 PM
Evening Walk & Dinner
A longer walk before dinner. I take extra time in the evenings — it's the best part of a dog's day and mine too.
05
9:00 PM
Bedtime Wind-Down
Final potty trip, last check-in photo, and settling in for the night. Your dog sleeps exactly where they always do.
06
Ongoing
Medication & Special Care
Senior dogs, anxious dogs, special diets — none of it is extra trouble. It's just part of knowing the dog properly.
