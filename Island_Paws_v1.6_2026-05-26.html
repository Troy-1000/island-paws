About us

A family that lives for this.

We're a young family on Bainbridge Island: two kids, a baby, and more dogs through our door than we can count. Every dog we care for is treated exactly like one of ours.

Daily routine

A typical day
with the pack.

6 – 8 am
Wake Up & Breakfast
Morning potty, fresh food, and a calm start. We make breakfast for everyone together.
8 am – 12 pm
Outdoor Time
The fenced yard is theirs. Ball throwing, pack play, garden roaming. Rain or shine.
12 – 4 pm
Lounge & Rest
Dogs follow us inside, supervise the kitchen, and find their spot among dedicated dog beds.
4 – 6 pm
Kids Come Home
Energy picks back up. Kids and dogs: the best combination for afternoon chaos and cuddles.
6 – 8 pm
Dinner & Wind Down
Evening meal, a movie, snuggles on the couch. Everyone settles in for a calm end to the day.
By 10 pm
Lights Out
Consistent bedtime, calm environment. Your dog always knows what comes next, just like home.
