Who We Are

A family home,
not a kennel

We have a large fenced yard in a quiet neighborhood in the center of Bainbridge Island where dogs run freely while we garden, work, and care for our home and kids. When your dog stays with us, they're truly part of the family.

Trenton has had dogs his whole life — Golden Retrievers, Australian Shepherds, Great Pyrenees, Shihpoos. He loves the nuances of different breeds. Jordan brings deep empathy and devotional care to every dog in our home. Our three kids (ages 1, 8 & 10) absolutely adore every visitor.

We can accommodate special needs dogs, senior dogs, and all sizes from teacup to giant. Just let us know what your pup needs.

Daily Routine

A typical day
with the pack

6 – 8 am
Wake Up & Breakfast
Morning potty, fresh food, and a calm start. We make breakfast for everyone — the whole pack — together.
8 am – 12 pm
Outdoor Time
The fenced yard is theirs. Ball throwing, pack play, garden roaming — rain or shine (mostly ☀️).
12 – 4 pm
Lounge & Rest
Dogs follow us around inside, supervise the kitchen, and find their spot among dedicated dog furniture and beds.
4 – 6 pm
Kids Come Home
Energy picks back up. Kids and dogs — the best combination for afternoon chaos and cuddles.
6 – 8 pm
Dinner & Wind Down
Evening meal, a movie, snuggles on the couch. Everyone settles in for a calm, comfortable end to the day.
By 10 pm
Lights Out
Consistent bedtime, calm environment. Your dog always knows what comes next — just like home.
